Cybersecurity

What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity refers to the practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks. These cyberattacks are usually aimed at accessing, changing, or destroying sensitive information, extorting money from users, or interrupting normal business processes.

Why Learn Cybersecurity?

Learning Cybersecurity is crucial for several reasons:

  • Protects sensitive data
  • Addresses growing threats in the digital world
  • Creates opportunities in a high-demand field
  • Builds a strong foundation in digital safety and protection

Roadmap to Learn Cybersecurity

  1. Understand basic networking concepts (TCP/IP, DNS, HTTP/S)
  2. Learn about operating systems (Windows, Linux)
  3. Study common cybersecurity threats and vulnerabilities
  4. Get familiar with security tools and technologies (firewalls, antivirus, IDS/IPS)
  5. Explore ethical hacking and penetration testing
  6. Understand cryptography and data protection methods
  7. Stay updated with the latest cybersecurity trends and best practices
