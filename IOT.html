Internet of Things (IoT)

What is Internet of Things (IoT)?

Internet of Things (IoT) refers to the network of physical devices embedded with sensors, software, and other technologies that enable them to connect and exchange data with other devices and systems over the internet. These devices can collect and transmit data without human intervention, leading to automation and improved efficiency in various industries.

Why Learn Internet of Things (IoT)?

Learning IoT is crucial for several reasons:

  • High demand for IoT professionals in various industries
  • Enables you to work on innovative projects involving smart devices
  • Opportunities to improve efficiency and automation in different sectors
  • Builds a strong foundation in networking, programming, and data analysis

Roadmap to Learn Internet of Things (IoT)

  1. Understand basic IoT concepts and architecture
  2. Learn about sensors, actuators, and microcontrollers (Arduino, Raspberry Pi)
  3. Study networking protocols used in IoT (MQTT, CoAP, HTTP)
  4. Get familiar with IoT platforms and cloud services (AWS IoT, Azure IoT)
  5. Explore data collection, storage, and analysis techniques
  6. Work on real-world IoT projects and applications
  7. Stay updated with the latest trends and advancements in IoT technology
