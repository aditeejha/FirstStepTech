About FirstStepTech

FirstStepTech is a beginner-friendly platform designed to help students explore different tech skills without feeling overwhelmed.

It provides clear explanations, structured roadmaps, and curated resources so beginners can understand what a skill is, why it matters, and how to start learning it.

The goal is to simplify the first step into technology and remove the confusion that often comes with choosing a learning path.

Why This Platform Exists

  • To help beginners choose the right tech skill confidently
  • To provide clear starting points instead of scattered information
  • To make learning technology structured and approachable
